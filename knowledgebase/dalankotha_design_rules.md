# Dalankotha Design Rules
*Last Updated: 2026*

Core aesthetic and structural philosophies for the Ghorbari platform. The NanoBanana Image Prompts generated must align with these directives.

## The Aesthetic Core
- **Dalankotha Geometry:** Clean lines, engineered perfection, structured brickwork. Avoid overly ornate or cluttered rustic Victorian styles unless explicitly requested by the user.
- **Color Palette Integration:** Focus on natural earthy tones (Titas Gas blue-grey, brick terracotta red, Sundarban emerald green hues).
- **Practicality over Fantasy:** All designs must look physically buildable in the context of urban Dhaka/Chittagong conditions (e.g. realistic window grilles for security, damp-proof concrete baselines, sun-shading chajjas).
