# Content Strategy Skill: The Authority Engine (Ghorbari Edition)

**Objective:** To dominate the "Trust Space" in Bangladesh’s construction market by converting technical engineering expertise into high-engagement, empathetic content that drives high-value leads.

---

## 1. The Strategy Framework: PIE (Problem, Insight, Execution)
Every piece of content generated under this skill must follow the **PIE** model, grounded in the **Owl & Eagle** logic:
*   **P (Problem):** Identify a specific construction or financial pain point. (e.g., "দামি রড কিনছেন, কিন্তু মরিচা ধরা থেকে বাঁচাবেন কীভাবে?") — **[The Owl sees the hidden risk]**.
*   **I (Insight):** Provide a technical "aha!" moment backed by engineering data. (e.g., "It’s not just the grade; it’s the storage and the concrete cover.") — **[The Sage provides Technical Authority]**.
*   **E (Execution):** A clear, actionable next step. (e.g., "Download our Material Storage Guide" or "Book a free site audit.") — **[The Eagle sees the high-level vision]**.

## 4. The Trust Funnel (Content Journey)

### **Phase 1: Awareness (The Hook - Awareness)**
*   **Goal:** Capture attention in 2 seconds via "Myth-Busting" or "Market Price Alerts."
*   **Content Pillars:** Market Watch, Myth Buster.
*   *Example:* "৩টি ভুলের কারণে আপনার বাড়ির কলামে ফাটল দেখা দিতে পারে।" (Anxiety/Authority Hook)

### **Phase 2: Consideration (The Proof - Authority)**
*   **Goal:** Prove technical expertise through "Value Engineering" and Site Audits.
*   **Content Pillars:** Value Engineering, Site Visits.
*   *Example:* "How we saved BDT 50,000 on a 1200sqft slab design through structural optimization."

### **Phase 3: Decision (The Offer - Action)**
*   **Goal:** Remove final friction with "Transparency" and "Fixed Commission" offers.
*   **Content Pillars:** Case Studies, Testimonials, Renovation Journey.
*   *Example:* "Book a 15-minute Structural Consultation for free."

## 5. Platform Adaptation & Language Logic
*   **Language Mix:** 
    *   **90% Pure/Simple Bangla** for clarity and technical trust.
    *   **10% Technical Terms** (e.g., *Curing, FAR, AAC Block*) ALWAYS followed by a simple explanation.
    *   *Urban/Banglish:* Used strategically for High-End Interior/Upgrader personas on Instagram.
*   **Visual Standards:**
    *   Use **Dark Blue (Trust)**, **Green (Growth)**, and **Orange (Energy)**.
    *   Prioritize **Real Site Photos** & Human-Centric visuals (Engineers in safety helmets).
    *   Logo placement: Header corner or bottom-center.

## 7. Operational Directives for the Agent
**When executing the 'Content Skill', the Agent must:**
1.  **Balance the Mix:** Follow the 60/30/10 Rule: 60% Educational, 30% Authority, 10% Sales.
2.  **Describe Visual Context:** Always provide a visual prompt for the designer (e.g., "Show a cross-section of a footing with proper concrete cover").
3.  **Revenue Check:** Every piece of content must contribute to the target (e.g., "Does this move a lead with a BDT 10 Lac+ budget?").
4.  **Authority Check:** Use "Material Forensic" logic (e.g., Mention specific tests like 'Field Test for Bricks').
5.  **Community Vibe:** In comments, be the "Trusted Brother"—helpful, not salesy. (e.g., "জ্বি ভাই, আমরা আপনার স্বপ্নপূরণে পাশে আছি")।
