## 4. Visual & Verbal Identity Audit
* **Tone Consistency:** Does the brand sound the same on WhatsApp as it does on a Billboard?
* **Visual Recall:** Are the signature colors (Dark Blue/Green/Orange) applied consistently across all touchpoints?
* **Sentiment Analysis:** Common keywords used by customers in mentions (e.g., "Reliable," "Expensive but worth it," "Fast response").

---

## 5. Performance Metrics (KPIs)
* **Brand Awareness:** Monthly website traffic and social media reach.
* **Brand Trust:** Percentage of leads coming from "Word of Mouth" vs. "Paid Ads."
* **Conversion Efficiency:** Average time from "First Inquiry" to "Contract Signing."

---

## 6. Strategic Gap Analysis (The "Owl" Perspective)
* **Unmet Need:** What is the customer asking for that no one is providing? (e.g., "Post-construction maintenance subscription").
* **Communication Gap:** Is the technical jargon confusing the "Apartment Upgrader" persona?

## 7. Agent Operational Instructions
**When performing a Brand Analysis, you must:**
1.  **Be Objective:** Highlight the flaws as clearly as the successes.
2.  **Use Real Data:** Look for current material price trends and actual customer feedback.
3.  **Provide Actionable "Eagle" Moves:** Don't just analyze; suggest the next 3 steps to improve the brand's position.

## 10. Offer & Funnel Gaps

* **Pricing Clusters**: [Observed pricing tiers, anchor prices used, and price elasticity signals in the current market. Do we have a low-barrier entry price (e.g., 'Design Audit')?].
* **Offer Stacking**: [Bundles, cross-sells, down-sells (or lack thereof). (e.g., 'Stacking design fee + initial soil test + RAJUK guide at a bundle discount')].
* **Friction Points**: [UX or funnel drop-offs identified. Where are leads being lost? (e.g., 'A highly complex online form' or 'No WhatsApp instant response')].
* **Trust Gaps**: [Where is trust broken in the customer journey? (e.g., 'Lack of recent project testimonials' or 'Vague material source information')].
