# 4-Week Strategic Content Plan: Ghorbari

## 🎯 Market Intelligence & Brand Analysis Output
*(Synthesized based on Competitor Landscape, Market Intelligence & Strategy Execution, and Brand Analysis guidelines)*

**1. Competitor & Gap Analysis:** 
*   **Competitor Focus:** Firms like Bengal Interiors and Sheraspace market heavily on aesthetics and turnkey convenience. Local unstructured "mistrys" sell entirely on low upfront costs. 
*   **The Unmet Need / Trust Gap:** Customers lack a transparent, engineered solution that guarantees safety and strict material estimation without hidden costs. 
*   **Ghorbari's Positioning (Moat):** High-Quality & Transparent Pricing Axis. While competitors offer standard interior design, we offer **"Standardized Execution, Laser-Accuracy, and Technical Certainty."**

**2. Core Emotional Triggers & Messaging:**
*   **Trigger:** **Safety/Security (Anti-Cheating).** The homeowner’s biggest fear is losing their life savings to inefficient materials, hidden costs, or regulatory compliance failures (RAJUK/DAP).
*   **Tone:** The "Sage/Advisor." We speak with 90% accessible Bangla and 10% technical authority (e.g., FAR, Yield Strength, Soft-Close hardware) to build the "Trusted Big Brother" persona.

**3. Macroeconomic Triggers (Agile Marketing):**
*   If Rod/Cement prices spike, immediately pivot content toward **"Value Engineering"**: *How Ghorbari saves 10-15% of your metal/steel weight through structural optimization without compromising safety.*
